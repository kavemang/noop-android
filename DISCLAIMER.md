Metrics such as recovery, strain, HRV, sleep, stress, respiratory rate, SpO2, skin temperature, and related summaries are approximate self-tracking signals. Do not rely on them for medical decisions.

NOOP Android is independent and unofficial. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to WHOOP, Inc. "WHOOP" is used only to identify compatible hardware.
